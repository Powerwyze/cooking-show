HOW TO PLAY
THE BASICS
🌾
Collect Ingredients

Click any ingredient in the grid to add it to your inventory. You need to collect all the required ingredients for a recipe before you can cook it.

⚗️
Craft Special Items

Some recipes require crafted ingredients like Pasta or Dough. Select a crafting recipe in the Craft Station — it'll consume two base ingredients from your inventory and produce the crafted item.

📖
Pick a Recipe

Click any recipe card to see what it needs. If you have all the ingredients AND the required equipment in your kitchen, the ✓ READY badge lights up and you can cook it.

🍳
Equipment Matters

Each recipe requires a specific piece of kitchen equipment (Pan, Oven, Wok…). You start with 3 random tools. When you advance a stage, your equipment is refreshed — so plan ahead!

COOKING MINI-GAMES

Each cooking step triggers one of three mini-games. You have a countdown timer per step — fail and your ingredients are lost!

🔪
MASH Rapid Clicking

A big emoji target appears. Click it as fast as you can the required number of times before time runs out. Harder recipes need more clicks. Actions: Chop, Beat, Whip, Form, Drain, Garnish.

🎯
TIMING Hit the Zone

A ball bounces back and forth on a track. Press TAP when it's inside the green zone. Miss = time penalty. The zone gets narrower on harder recipes. Actions: Flip, Sear, Bake, Slice, Caramelize, Toast, Rest, Fold, Finish.

🌡️
HOLD Temperature Control

Hold the button to raise a temperature gauge. Release to let it fall. Keep it in the green zone for 2 continuous seconds to succeed. Everything else uses this: Heat, Simmer, Stir, Season, Fry, Cook, and more.

SCORING & PROGRESSION
⭐
Judge Ratings

Three judges rate your dish 1–5 stars. Each judge has a specialty — Gordon loves French cuisine, Martha prefers American classics, Bobby is all about street food. Match their tastes for higher scores.

🏆
Stage Progression

Finishing any recipe advances you to the next stage. Each stage unlocks one brand-new recipe and refreshes your kitchen equipment. There are 35 recipes to unlock total — can you master them all?
